The Work

For Nike Air Max Day, Pinterest partnered with Nike to launch a custom Pin Extension. A collage-style interactive experience where Pinners could design personalized posters using Nike quotes, images, and textures, with an integrated product discovery step highlighting Air Max styles.

The Outcome

Through strategic art direction, seamless user flow, and supporting auction media, Nike Air Max day was celebrated by bridging creative engagement with shoppable inspiration.
