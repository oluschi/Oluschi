Nova

Branding + Filter 2021.

Nova Ray's Diner is a restaurant is geared towards the reminiscent experiences of an Southern Diners through a simulated virtual experience. The way food will not be consumed in a literal sense, but through a VR experience, where users can be anywhere in the galaxy to experience the nostalgia.

Influence behind the Diner:
50s- Dinner aesthetic
60s- Influence of Space Imagery/ Cold War
70s- Rise of Afro Futurism and Black Pride
80s- The use of Neon colors and the depiction of space in film
