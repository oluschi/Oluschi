My name is Oluschi Harmon , and I'm a graduate from Parsons School of Design in NYC, with a Bachelor's degree in Communication Design and a minor in Fashion Communication.

As a graphic designer, my philosophy is centered around a quote by Elsie de Wolfe “I am going to make everything around me beautiful, that will be my life”.

I aim to create visually compelling designs that not only meet the needs of my clients but also enrich the lives of those who encounter them. I invite you to take a look at my design portfolio, which showcases my skills and creativity. Thank you for your time and consideration.
