Museum Design

Type Design 2021.

For this project we where task with developing branding for a museum concept of our choice. The project consist of creating a logo, 2 posters; a main museum and a event poster, and a website mockup. I choose to make a museum that commmemorates some of London’s icon fashion designers that emerged from King’s Road.


Dynamic Word + Mantra Merch

Type Design + Gifs, 2020.

For this project, I choose the mantra “Smile For Me”. inspired by the hit 2000s song “Grillz” by Nelly. From this mantra I went on to create merchandise and gifs. The gifs where made using Adobe AfterEffects inorder to create the rotation and look of my design.


Interview with a Designer

Type Design + Collage, 2021.

In this project, we were task with creating a 12 page book layout from an interview we conducted. For my project, I interviewed a young fashion designer and talked about her experience as a women of color within her industry. I wanted the book to be reminiscent of the aesthetic of y2k magazine with a modern feel.

Trend Forecasting: Mimi Wade

Digital Collage, 2021.

For my Fashion Trend Analysis class we were task in developing trend boards form a company of our choice using information from trend forecasting sites such as WGSN and Stylus.The project focused on creating boards based off the upcoming trends for season of Spring’22, that incorprates the overall aesthetic of the brand. For my project, I choose to do the designer Mimi Wade and her self-named brand. Through my research I was able to develop 4 future forecast boards that may be utilized for this brand.
